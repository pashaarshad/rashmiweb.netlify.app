About Me

Discover my journey and passions

A Dedicated Librarian & Literary Writer

Rashmi K. Vishwanath is a distinguished librarian at Sheshadripuram College in Mysore, Karnataka. She is a passionate literary writer with extensive experience in writing and reading poems, essays, stories, and short verses (Hanigavana).

Her commitment extends beyond literature to social service, including blood and hair donation, and active participation in community programs. As an accomplished anchor and traveler, she brings diverse experiences to her literary work.

With qualifications in ITI in Computer Operator & Programming Assistant, M.L.I Sc, and M.A in Kannada, Rashmi has a strong foundation of knowledge and skills that she shares through her various platforms and literary contributions.
